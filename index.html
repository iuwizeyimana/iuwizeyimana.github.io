Hello!

My name is Iris; I am a PhD candidate in Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto, where my research focuses on sustainable machine learning systems. Broadly, I study how to model, analyze, and optimize the performance, energy, and carbon impact of emerging computing systems, from large-scale data centers to heterogeneous edge platforms.

Before my PhD, I completed a MASc in Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto, where I worked on compiler optimization for deep neural network accelerators. I am especially interested in building practical modeling tools and hardware mapping optimizations that help make modern ML systems more efficient and sustainable.
